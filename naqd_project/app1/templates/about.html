About Us

At Naqd, we understand that managing finances as a freelancer or small business owner can be a complex and time-consuming challenge. That’s why we've created a tailored platform designed to simplify and streamline your payment and debt management processes. Whether you're handling cash, bank transfers, or cheques, Naqd offers a comprehensive solution to help you efficiently manage and track your customer payments, minimize late fees, and ensure smooth cash flow.


Our Mission

Our goal is to empower freelancers and small business owners by providing them with the tools and resources they need to effectively manage their debts, payments, and financial obligations. We believe that every entrepreneur deserves a straightforward, reliable, and secure way to track transactions and get paid on time — and that’s exactly what Naqd delivers.

Why Choose Naqd?

  • Tailored for Freelancers & Small Businesses: We specialize in solutions that are flexible and scalable, so they fit your unique business needs.
  • Multi-Payment Support: No matter how your customers pay — whether by cash, bank transfer, or cheque — Naqd keeps everything in one place.
  • Time-Saving Automation: Automate your payment tracking and reminders to save valuable time and reduce manual effort.
  • Simple & Intuitive: Naqd is designed with ease of use in mind, so you can get started quickly, without the need for complicated software or training.
